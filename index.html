About Us

FRIENDS OF INDIA z. s. is an association whose mission is to support the development, enrichment and improvement of relations and life between India and the Czech Republic in cultural, social, educational and economic areas.

Main Activities

  • Organizing thematic events and meetings of members and guests to exchange experiences, support common interests, and establish personal and professional contacts.
  • Building and developing relations between the Czech Republic and India including cooperation with partner cultural, social and economic organizations.
  • Initiating activities aimed at strengthening international relations and free exchange of people and information between the Czech Republic and India at civic, corporate and governmental levels.
  • Supporting research, education, cooperation with universities, development of culture and other activities aligned with the association’s goals.

Goal of the Association

The goal of the association is cooperation with entrepreneurs, government authorities and institutions in order to strengthen mutually beneficial relations and contacts between the Czech Republic and India.

Added Value

The added value of FRIENDS OF INDIA z. s. is active development of contacts, strengthening international relations and supporting long-term cooperation between Czech and Indian partners.
