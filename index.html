About the Association

FRIENDS OF INDIA z. s. supports the development, enrichment and improvement of relations between India and the Czech Republic in cultural, social, educational and economic areas.

Main Activities

Community Events

Organizing meetings, cultural events and discussions for members and guests.

International Cooperation

Developing relationships between organizations and institutions in India and Czech Republic.

Education & Research

Supporting universities, research cooperation and cultural exchange.

Goal of the Association

The association cooperates with entrepreneurs, government authorities and institutions to strengthen mutually beneficial relations between the Czech Republic and India.
